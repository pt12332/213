About Us

We are Topdawg Restaurant, a family-owned and operated business that has been serving delicious food to the community for over 20. In 1999, a young man named Chris founded HotDawg with a dream of creating the best hot dog restaurant. Today, he has achieved his dream, and HotDawg has become a well-known restaurant throughout the USA. It's inspiring to see someone pursue their passion and turn it into a successful business. Hot dogs are a popular food item in the United States, and it's great to see a restaurant dedicated to perfecting them.
